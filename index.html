ABOUT GREEN GLOBAL

Incorporated in 2014

Green Global (GG) Group through its Green Global Facilities Management (GGFM) specialises in providing comprehensive soft services solutions in the Total Facilities Management (TFM) domain, Eco Green Global Technical Services (EGGTS) aspires to serve the hard services segment in the TFM arena.

Services provided by GG encompass, administrative support services, landscaping maintenance, pest control, waste management, marble crystallisation & vitrification, rope access & high rise window cleaning, hotel & concierge services, industrial & warehouse cleaning, carpet & upholstery among others.

EGGTS, Dubai & GGFM, Abu Dhabi, with their own expertise and regional focus, strategically are able to service the entire UAE market.

GG is committed to satisfy its customers and strive to excel in fulfilling their needs. Emphasis on excellence, quality is bolstered to ensure that professional decorum is maintained at all times.

High quality, hygiene, appearance of its workforce is of paramount importance. GG is committed towards integrating green, ecological & environmental friendly practices adopted and continously improved.

Marble Floor Crystallisation & Vitrification

Vetrification is the process applied to the surface of natural calcareous flooring such as terrazzo or conglomerate marble. With the right combination of chemicals and application wearing and slip resistant surface, which invariably reduces maintenance and daily cleaning and maintenance. GGFM Vetrification will immensely improve the appearance of marble floors thus creating a flawless spotless finish.
